Sunset Peak

Hong Kong

Lantau Island

Lies to the west of Hong Kong Island, is dominated by two enormous mountains — the 934-metre-high Lantau Peak and 869-metre Sunset Peak, which are respectively the city’s second and third highest peaks. The latter offers incredible views of the ocean, islands and beaches, as well as beguiling silvergrass that grows on its slopes. A hike up this peak will also take you to the stone huts of a mysterious mountaintop settlement. @discoverhongkong.com
