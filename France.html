Palace of Versailles

Museum in Versailles, France

Palace of Versailles

The Palace had become an archetypal seat of power and continued to host the most important events in the history of France, such as the declaration of the German Empire, signed in 1871 following France's defeat at war by the Prussians.

Best Way to Visit?

Avoid queues

Buy a Passport ticket with timed entry to avoid the queues.

Time control

You'll need around an hour and a half for your visit. If you are early, enjoy a coffee at Ore or wander in the bookshop librairie des Prince while waiting for your visit to start.

Ate before visit

Food and drinks are not allowed inside the museum; picnics are not allowed in the gardens. However, it is possible to have a picnic in the park.
